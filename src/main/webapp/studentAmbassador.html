Role of a Student Ambassador

As a Student Ambassador (SA) of the Sustainable Education Foundation (SEF), you will act as a liaison for SEF while also acting as an Evangelist for the SEF brand among your peers. SA’s are trained and mentored throughout the program to enable you to be career-ready. This position is for student leaders who are passionate about developing their employability skills and sharing knowledge.
